**VETTING DOSSIER: K01628.03**  

**SIGNAL**  
K01628.03 is a candidate exoplanet with a 37.84-day orbit, 1.76 Earth radii, and equilibrium temperature of 607 K.  

**ASSESSMENT**  
The planet hypothesis is strongly supported: calibrated probability = 1.000, high transit SNR (23.7), and low centroid offset (0.8636σ) rule out most false positives. The small radius ratio (0.01404) and consistent odd-even depths (0.1663) further disfavor an eclipsing binary. Three transits on the same star bolster reliability.  

**FOLLOW-UP**  
High-resolution imaging to exclude unresolved background binaries near the target star would confirm the planetary nature.
