**SIGNAL**  
K01145.01 is a candidate planet with a 30.59-day orbit, 2.32 Earth radii, and equilibrium temperature of 621 K.  

**ASSESSMENT**  
The planet hypothesis is strongly supported: the calibrated probability (1.000) and conformal set ("CONFIRMED") indicate high confidence.
